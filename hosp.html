WARM RECEPTION

Hospitality is the bedrock of any social event. It’s the very base, intrinsic aspect, experiential mettle, core value of what sets apart one event from the other. Like salt to a dish… it has to be there and in perfect proportion!


Our belief in according this aspect due regard and attention has made us deliver many exceptional events.


The bouquet of services pan across from the host to the guest – from helping the Host family in hotel selection and bookings, guest list preparation and co-ordination, to room allocation and detailed, thematic check in at the hotel.









A dedicated Hospitality team tends to each and every query that a guest might have and assists in all manner of logistics, transport , gift placements and medical and security alerts, concierge services, at the hotels and venues. Airport management keeps our outstation guests in capable hands.


Thematic, concept-based collaterals keep the communication channel interesting and clear. From creating website and apps, integrated with social media, to proxy the host family and make our guests feel welcomed and cared for at all times.


The Hospitality extends to all facets of an event… especially in leaving the right aftertaste…


Detailed food & beverage planning done by our expert team helps in identifying and curating the right epicurean experience that has a 360 degree consideration of menu identification, bar management, food layouting, service detailing and menu creation.
